About Us

The Association of Heads of Independent Schools of Australia (AHISA) is a professional association for principals of independent schools across Australia. Members’ schools include primary / secondary day and boarding, and are of religious and non-denominational affiliations

For more information about AHISA please visit our website

The Event

AHISA is proud to present the 14th AHISA Biennial Conference from Monday 30th September to Wednesday 2nd October 2013 to be held at the Grand Hyatt, Melbourne. The Biennial is AHISA's premier conference event, highly valued by members as an opportunity for Australia's independent school leaders to gather together for professional and personal exchange.

Download the AHISA 2013 Biennial Conference Program

The AHISA Biennial Conference is a members-only event. It provides the platform for professional exchange between peers who aspire to and are recognised for excellence in autonomous school leadership.

The Venue

The Grand Hyatt Melbourne hotel is a recently refurbished 5-star hotel on Collins Street in the centre of the CBD. The entire second floor is dedicated to conferencing — ensuring that all exhibition space, conference rooms and catering is centrally located allowing for full exposure of delegates to exhibitors during all breaks. The exhibition will be held in the Mayfair Ballroom.

This conference will provide unique opportunities to present products and services to the key decision makers from Australian independent schools.
